Raw Leonardite Ore

Unprocessed leonardite ore for slow-release humic and fulvic acid amendment in garden soil. Leonardite is an oxidized form of lignite coal, naturally rich in humic substances that have accumulated over millions of years. As raw ore decomposes in soil contact and moisture, it gradually releases humic and fulvic acids into the soil matrix. This slow-release profile makes raw ore ideal for building long-term soil organic matter without the intensity of processed powders.
