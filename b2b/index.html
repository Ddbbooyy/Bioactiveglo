B2B & Wholesale Partnerships

Wholesale pricing, white-label manufacturing, co-branding opportunities, and ingredient supply for qualified retail, manufacturing, and distribution partners.

Partnership Options

Three partnership structures designed to fit different business models and scale requirements.

🏷

White-Label

BioActiveGlo products manufactured to our quality and specification standards, labeled with your brand identity. Maintain your brand while leveraging our formulation expertise and supply chain.

  • ✓ Your branding, our formulation
  • ✓ Full specification documentation
  • ✓ COA provided per batch
  • ✓ Gardening-only use required
🤝

Co-Branding

Joint branding on products sold under both your brand and BioActiveGlo. Ideal for retailers and partners who want the credibility of the BioActiveGlo formulation name alongside their own brand identity.

  • ✓ Dual brand presence
  • ✓ Access to BioActiveGlo marketing assets
  • ✓ Educational content support
  • ✓ Shared market credibility
🧪

Ingredient Supply

Bulk fulvic acid, humic acid, and leonardite ore supplied as raw ingredients for your own formulation and manufacturing process. Verified composition and full traceability documentation.

  • ✓ Bulk powder and ore supply
  • ✓ Composition-verified batches
  • ✓ COA and source documentation
  • ✓ Volume pricing available
