Humic acid is a naturally occurring organic compound formed over millions of years from ancient organic matter in soil. It's studied for its ability to bind heavy metals, environmental toxins, and metabolic waste in the gut — supporting detoxification and creating a cleaner environment for nutrient absorption. Think of it as nature's filter for your digestive system.
